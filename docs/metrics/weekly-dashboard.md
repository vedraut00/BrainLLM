## North Star

> **Magic number = Skills approved per active org.**
>
> This is the single number that proves the product is *working*, not just *signed up*. An org that keeps approving SKILL.md files is an org getting value (their AI agents stop hallucinating). An org sitting at 0 approved skills will churn no matter how nice the demo was.
>
> Everything else (calls, demos, MRR) is upstream or downstream of this. If "skills approved / active org" is climbing week over week, you have a real business. If it's flat near zero, fix the product/onboarding before spending another rupee on outreach.
>
> **Target trajectory:** ≥1 by first paid week, ≥3 by Week 13, ≥5 = sticky.

## How to compute each metric

| Metric | Definition / how to count |
|--------|---------------------------|
| **Discovery calls booked** | Count of *new* discovery/qualification calls that landed on the calendar this week (booked, not necessarily held). Source: calendar + CRM. |
| **Demos done** | Live product demos actually *held* this week (not booked). One per prospect org. |
| **Demo→pilot %** | `(Pilots started this week ÷ Demos done this week) × 100`. Rough conversion read; fine if it swings early. |
| **Pilots started** | Orgs that began a free pilot / design-partner trial this week (connected a source, started onboarding). |
| **Pilot→paid %** | `(Pilots that converted to paid this week ÷ Pilots eligible to convert) × 100`. "Eligible" = pilots that have reached their decision point. |
| **Weekly-active orgs** | Distinct orgs that did a *value action* in the last 7 days — approved/edited a skill, hit the MCP server, or synced a source. **NOT** just logged in. This is your retention pulse. |
| **Skills approved / active org** | `(Total SKILL.md files in "approved" status across active orgs) ÷ (Weekly-active orgs)`. **← North Star.** Count only human-approved, current-version skills. |
| **MRR (USD)** | Sum of monthly-equivalent recurring revenue from paying orgs. Annual plans = `annual ÷ 12`. Apply the 50%-for-life design-partner discount to their line. Exclude one-off / not-yet-charged. |
| **Churn** | Orgs that cancelled or stopped paying this week. Log as count + lost $MRR, e.g. `1 org / -$99`. Also note "soft churn" (active→inactive) in Notes — it predicts paid churn. |
| **Notes** | One honest line: biggest win, biggest blocker, or the gate decision. This is for *you* in 3 months. |

> **Tip:** Pre-revenue (Weeks 1–8), `MRR = $0` is expected — don't let blank MRR discourage you. The leading indicators are **Weekly-active orgs** and **Skills approved/active org**. If those move, revenue follows.

## 🚦 Phase Gates — decision checkpoints

- **Week 2 — Validation gate:** Of ~15 wedge prospects (CS/support leaders at 20–200-person SaaS/D2C/agencies) you spoke to, **≥5 say they would pay**. If fewer, **stop and re-cut the wedge/message** before writing more code.
- **Weeks 7–8 — Beta gate:** **≥3 design partners active *every* week** AND verbally committed to pay. If partners signed up but went inactive, the product isn't delivering the magic number yet — fix activation, don't chase logos.
- **Weeks 9–10 — FIRST REVENUE:** First real invoices paid. Even at the 50%-for-life rate, **money must change hands.** A "yes I'd pay" that hasn't been charged is not validated.
- **Weeks 11–13 — Scale milestone:** **~10 paying customers** and **first hundreds of $ MRR**, with weekly-active usage holding. This is the proof point for the next phase.

## 💰 Funding-readiness reminder

> **Bootstrap first. Do not raise on a deck.**
>
> - **Bootstrap to $2–5k MRR before raising.** As a solo India-based founder selling globally in USD, your costs are low — $2–5k MRR is real runway *and* the proof a serious investor wants. Use this window to keep equity and learn what actually retains.
> - **Accelerator / pre-seed unlocks when you have ALL of:**
>   - ✅ A **working product** (not a prototype — partners use it unprompted)
>   - ✅ **5–10 paying customers**
>   - ✅ **Weekly active usage** (your North Star — skills approved/active org — climbing)
> - Until those three are true, your highest-leverage move is **more discovery calls + tighter activation**, not fundraising. Revisit this block at the **Week 13** milestone.
